<h1>Grimmerie Tradition Spells</h1>

<p class="flavor-text">"The Grimmerie speaks in tongues of fire and shadow, accessible only to those willing to pay its price."</p>

<p>The Grimmerie Tradition represents the most powerful and dangerous arcane magic in Oz. These spells are primarily drawn from <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('objects/magic-items/artifacts/the-grimmerie', 'the Grimmerie'); return false;">the Grimmerie</a> itself, the ancient book of forbidden magic that only a few—like <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('npcs/elphaba-thropp', 'Elphaba Thropp'); return false;">Elphaba</a>—have successfully decoded. These spells are available primarily to <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/classes/the-witch', 'Witches'); return false;">Witches</a> and <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/classes/the-adept', 'Adepts'); return false;">Adepts</a> who have studied the text.</p>

<h2>Accessing Grimmerie Spells</h2>
<p>Unlike standard arcane magic, Grimmerie spells often require:</p>
<ul>
<li>Physical access to the Grimmerie or copied pages</li>
<li>Successful Intelligence (Arcana) checks to decode (DC varies by spell level)</li>
<li>Personal sacrifice or cost beyond normal spell components</li>
<li>Risk of corruption (see <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('magic/phenomena/grimmerie-corruption', 'Grimmerie Corruption'); return false;">Grimmerie Corruption</a>)</li>
</ul>
