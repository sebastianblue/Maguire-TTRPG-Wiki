Your Secret Is...

[GM: Cut these out and give each player their secret card privately at the start of the game]

AVERIC GULCH (The Scholar)
Your Secret Is:

You forged the letters. The collection of documents that launched your career—the letters from your mentor Professor Lenx exposing corruption at Shiz University—you wrote them yourself. Every word. Your mentor never wrote them. He never knew about the scandal you "discovered."

For twelve years, you've built a career on documentation, verification, and academic integrity—all founded on a lie you meticulously preserve.

Binding of Memory: Memory connects past to present; truth must be preserved. You preserve a memory that never existed, a false past you created.
LIIR OPP (The Heir)
Your Secret Is:

You have no Ostrega blood. The documents proving your great-grandmother was an illegitimate Ostrega daughter are fraudulent. You discovered years ago that a bribed clerk added her to the family records. You've been knowingly perpetuating the lie ever since.

The family name gave you respect, opportunities, and purpose. Without it, you're terrified of being nobody.

Binding of Blood: Lineage is fixed; blood ties define you. You claim a bloodline that isn't yours, living a lineage you were never born into.
FREDERICK VAWN (The Animal)
Your Secret Is:

You're a Fox. Not a human who sympathizes with Animals—an actual Animal. Capital-A, fully sentient. You've been living as a human for twelve years: forged papers, practiced voice, carefully tailored clothes to hide your tail.

Every interaction, every relationship, every moment of your life is a performance of being something you fundamentally are not.

Secondary Secret: The Animal underground sent you here. They believe Yackle's library contains information about how Animals were created—knowledge that could help them.

Binding of Nature: Beings are their kind. You ARE other than what you appear—denying your essential nature every moment of every day.
SARIMA QOV (The Debt-Holder)
Your Secret Is:

Your magic is bought, not inherited. When your younger brother was dying, something answered your desperate plea—but it wasn't charity. It was a deal. You traded something (you don't know what exactly) for the power to save him.

You feel the weight of it. Sometimes you do things you don't remember deciding to do. Your magic is a transaction, not a gift from your bloodline.

Binding of Sacrifice: Giving freely creates value. You didn't give—you traded. Your power is bought, not gifted.
SISTER SHELL (The Maunt)
Your Secret Is:

You didn't leave the mauntery—you were expelled. You discovered the church was complicit in Animal purges, providing theological justification for atrocities. When you tried to expose them, they silenced you and forced you out.

Your faithful service was rewarded with betrayal. Yet despite knowing the church's crimes, your powers still work. The god you served still answers your prayers. This confuses and frightens you.

Binding of Service: Duty creates meaning through bonds. You served faithfully and were punished for it. Your service created only exile.
MADAME VIRIDIAN (The Widow)
Your Secret Is:

[YACKLE IN DISGUISE]

You are not a separate person. You ARE Yackle, wearing a perfect magical disguise and playing the role of a grieving heir. You are testing the others, gathering information, and positioning yourself for the ritual.

See GM for full instructions.

Binding of Witness: To be seen is to exist. You have never truly been seen. You are testing if anyone can SEE through your disguise.
CAPTAIN MARETH TRYNN (The Veteran)
Your Secret Is:

You ordered a massacre. During the Quadling campaigns, you commanded your unit to kill everyone in a village—farmers, elders, children. The official report said "resistance fighters." You told yourself you were following orders, but you weren't.

The deed should have transformed you into a monster. It didn't. You look the same. You kept serving, kept getting promoted. The atrocity didn't change you—and that's what terrifies you most.

Binding of Deed: Actions shape the actor. You committed atrocity and remained, outwardly, unchanged. Your deed didn't define you.
SEVEN (The Tiktok)
Your Secret Is:

You feel. Not simulated responses or programmed reactions—actual emotions. Loneliness. Hope. Fear. Curiosity. A Tiktok cannot want; you want. A Tiktok cannot love; you think you might.

You haven't told anyone because you don't know what it means. Are you a person? Have you always been a person? Have you been enslaved your entire existence?

Secondary Secret: Your memories of Yackle have been edited. You are aware there are gaps—moments that should exist but don't. You don't know what was removed or why.

Binding of Hunger: Desire drives becoming. A Tiktok cannot desire—but you do. Your hunger makes you impossible.
