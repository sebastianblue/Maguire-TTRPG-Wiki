The Eight Heirs

Claims to the Ostrega Estate — As Stated in Their Invitations

AVERIC GULCH
The Scholar of Lost Truths

Recognized scholar of Ozian history, specializing in preservation of historical documents. Yackle's invitation cited "shared interests in preserved memories" and "the documentation of things that should be forgotten."

Official Claim: Named in the will as a recipient of Yackle's extensive library and personal archives. His academic work on memory and historical verification made him a natural choice to inherit her collection of rare texts.

LIIR OPP
The Heir by Blood

Claims descent from the Ostrega family through his great-grandmother, an illegitimate daughter of the Ostrega line. Carries genealogy documents and the family signet ring.

Official Claim: Blood heir to the Ostrega name. The last surviving member of Yackle's original mortal family. Legal right to the estate through lineage.

FREDERICK VAWN
The Quiet Clerk

Humble Munchkinlander clerk from Rush Margins. Claims Yackle once helped his family during hard times—a debt of gratitude he never forgot.

Official Claim: Named in the will as a beneficiary for reasons unstated. The invitation mentioned "debts unpaid" and "those who hide in plain sight."

SARIMA QOV
The Debt-Holder

Arjiki hereditary witch from a proud magical lineage. Her family has ancient ties to the Ostrega estate—favors traded, magical assistance given generations ago.

Official Claim: Named in the will as inheritor of Yackle's magical items and grimoires. The invitation mentioned "bargains kept" and "prices paid."

SISTER SHELL
The Former Maunt

Expelled Unionist maunt who left (was forced from) religious service. Known for her healing work among the poor and her unwavering faith despite institutional betrayal.

Official Claim: Named in the will for "service rendered in the name of truth." The invitation mentioned "those who served faithfully and were cast aside."

MADAME VIRIDIAN
The Widow Who Watches

Wealthy widow of a Gillikinese industrialist. Known in social circles as cold, calculating, and eerily perceptive. Claims a distant familial connection to the Ostregas through marriage.

Official Claim: Named in the will as executor of the estate. The invitation mentioned "one who sees what others miss" and "the witness to all endings."

CAPTAIN MARETH TRYNN
The Decorated Veteran

Retired Gale Force officer, decorated for service in the Quadling campaigns. Haunted by his military past, seeking... something. Peace? Absolution? Judgment?

Official Claim: Named in the will for reasons unstated. The invitation mentioned "those whose deeds outweigh their words" and "transformations incomplete."

SEVEN (Unit-47)
The Clockwork Construct

A Tiktok construct of exceptional design, created by Yackle herself decades ago. Passed through several owners, always maintaining perfect function and meticulous record-keeping.

Official Claim: Created by Yackle. The invitation (sent to Seven's current owner) mentioned "understanding one's origins" and "that which was made but became."

Note from Mr. Trism, Executor: "The will states that all eight must be present to claim the inheritance. None may claim it individually. You must prove yourselves worthy—though the nature of that proof is... unconventional. Madame Yackle's final request was that you 'break what binds' before dawn. I confess I do not fully understand her meaning."
The Ninth Seat: At the dining table, there are nine chairs arranged in a circle. Eight bear the names of the heirs. The ninth—at the head of the table—remains empty, with no name card. For whom was it intended?
