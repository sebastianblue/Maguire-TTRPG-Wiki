HANDOUTS BY LOCATION IN THE ESTATE

FOYER (Session Start)

‚è∞ WHEN: Immediately as session begins
üìç WHERE: Hand to one player to read aloud, or GM reads
Handout: 01-the-will.html
Purpose: Sets up the adventure premise, establishes the 8-hour time limit, and introduces the Seven Seals challenge.
Notes: Mr. Trism (the dead solicitor puppet) reads this. Players should understand they must break 7 seals before dawn or die.

CEMETERY (Grounds Floor)

‚è∞ WHEN: When players explore the cemetery
üìç WHERE: Can be given as they read grave markers, or discovered if they dig
Handout: 04-cemetery-epitaphs.html
Purpose: Foreshadowing and creepy atmosphere. Each epitaph describes a character archetype matching the 8 pregens.
Notes: These graves are already dug, waiting. Players can find their OWN epithets if they look carefully. Also contains symbols for Cabinet 2 lock (Skull ‚Üí Hourglass ‚Üí Feather).

üìç WHERE: Clipped to music stand or found on piano

Purpose: Shows the "original melody" that the Simple Wood music box must match.

‚è∞ WHEN: When players examine the notebook on the table
üìç WHERE: Found lying open beside the deck of cards

CABINET LABELS & PROPS SETUP

CABINET LABELS (Cut from Handout 08)

‚è∞ WHEN: Pre-session prep
üìç WHERE: Cut out and attach to/beneath 8 small boxes representing cabinets
Setup: Use 8 small boxes, bags, or containers. Attach brass plaque labels (Cabinet I through VIII). Place all cabinets in Yackle's Study area where players can see them but must unlock each one.

Unlock Requirements:
  1. I (Star-Lock): Key dropped by Animated Armor (first combat)
  2. II (Bone-Lock): Combination from Cemetery (Skull ‚Üí Hourglass ‚Üí Feather)
  3. III (Smoke-Glass): Must touch with fire/candle
  4. IV (Mirror): Speak one personal truth aloud
  5. V (Singing): Play Simple Wood music box near it (or hum original melody)
  6. VI (Blood-Sealed): Press hand to seal, claim Ostrega name (1d4 damage if lying)
  7. VII (Puzzle-Box): Key-card from Clockwork Sentinel enemy
  8. VIII (Impossible): Auto-opens when all 7 seals are broken

DURING-SESSION HANDOUT ORDER

  1. Session Start: Give The Will (handout 01)
  2. Cemetery: Give Epitaphs (handout 04) when explored
  3. Music Room: Give Sheet Music (handout 05) when piano examined
  4. Servant Quarters: Give Daily Rounds (handout 02) when notebook found
  5. Observatory: Telescope Note (handout 09) hidden inside telescope prop
  6. Each Seal Completed: Give corresponding reward card (handout 12)
  7. Each Cabinet Opened: Give loot cards (handout 12) and any story handouts
  8. Cabinet 6 Opened: Give Family Tree (handout 10)
  9. All 7 Seals Broken: Cabinet 8 auto-opens, give Last Letter (handout 03) + I LOVE U (handout 07) + reward cards
  10. Final Battle: Players use reward cards and True Name against Yackle
