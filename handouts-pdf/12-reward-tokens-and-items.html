SEAL REWARDS — Token Cards

[GM: Cut these out and give to players when they complete seals]

REROLL TOKEN
Seal 1: Memory — One Use
Effect: You may reroll one failed d20 roll and use the new result. This can be an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw.

Trigger: Use immediately after seeing the result of a d20 roll.

Flavor: "The past is fixed, but the present can be rewritten."
DEATH'S DENIAL
Seal 2: Blood — One Use
Effect: The first time you are reduced to 0 hit points (but not killed outright), you automatically stabilize without needing to make death saving throws.

Trigger: Automatic when you drop to 0 HP.

Flavor: "Blood remembers. Blood endures."
TRUE SIGHT TOKEN
Seal 3: Nature — One Use
Effect: You have advantage on one Wisdom (Insight) check. Additionally, for 1 minute after using this, you can see through illusions and detect shapechangers in their true form.

Trigger: Declare use before making an Insight check.

Flavor: "See what is, not what pretends to be."
SWIFT AID
Seal 4: Service — Rest of Session
Effect: For the rest of this game session, you can use the Help action as a bonus action instead of as an action.

Trigger: Always active after receiving this.

Flavor: "True service knows no hesitation."
LAST STRIKE
Seal 5: Sacrifice — One Use
Effect: When you are reduced to 0 hit points (but not killed outright), you can immediately make one weapon attack or cast one cantrip as a reaction before falling unconscious.

Trigger: Use when dropped to 0 HP.

Flavor: "What you give in your final moment defines you."
RESOLVE TOKEN
Seal 6: Deed — One Use
Effect: You may reroll one failed saving throw and use the new result.

Trigger: Use immediately after failing a saving throw but before consequences are resolved.

Flavor: "Facing the truth gives you the strength to face anything."
SECOND WIND
Seal 7: Hunger — One Use
Effect: As a bonus action, you can recover one expended Hit Die. Roll the die and add your Constitution modifier. Regain that many hit points.

Trigger: Use as a bonus action on your turn.

Flavor: "Knowing what you want gives you the will to take it."

CABINET REWARDS — Magic Items

[GM: Cut these out and give to players when they open cabinets]

POTION OF HEALING
Cabinet 1 — Consumable
Effect: As an action, drink this potion to regain 2d4+2 hit points.

Rarity: Common

"For the first blow struck. May it not be the last."
SCROLL OF PROTECTION
FROM EVIL AND GOOD
Cabinet 1 — Consumable, 1st Level
Effect: Use an action to read this scroll and cast the spell. For 10 minutes, certain creature types have disadvantage on attacks against you.

Spell Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
RING OF NECROTIC
RESISTANCE
Cabinet 2 — Ring, Uncommon
Effect: While wearing this ring, you have resistance to necrotic damage (take half damage from necrotic sources).

Attunement: Not required

"Death is patient. Be more patient than death."
CLOAK OF DISPLACEMENT
Cabinet 3 — Cloak, Uncommon
Effect: While wearing this cloak, you have a +1 bonus to AC. (For longer campaigns, use full Cloak of Displacement: attacks against you have disadvantage until you take damage.)

Attunement: Not required

"What's hidden can also hide you."
BOTTLE OF SMOKE
Cabinet 3 — Consumable
Effect: As an action, throw this bottle up to 30 feet. It shatters on impact and creates a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog (as Fog Cloud spell) for 10 minutes or until dispersed by wind.

One Use Only
AMULET OF PROOF
AGAINST DETECTION
Cabinet 4 — Amulet, Uncommon
Effect: While wearing this amulet, you can't be targeted by divination magic or perceived through magical scrying sensors.

Attunement: Not required

"The hardest lock to pick is the one you built yourself."
MIRROR SHARD
Cabinet 4 — Consumable
Effect: As an action, hold this up to see through one illusion or reveal one shapechanged creature's true form within 30 feet. The mirror shatters after use.

One Use Only
INSTRUMENT OF
THE BARDS
Cabinet 5 — Instrument, Uncommon
Effect: While playing this instrument, you gain a +2 bonus to Performance checks. Additionally, once per long rest, you can cast Cure Wounds (1st level) as an action.

Attunement: Requires proficiency with musical instruments
TUNING FORK (Silver)
Cabinet 5 — Material Component
Effect: This silver tuning fork is worth 250gp and can be used as a material component for spells like Plane Shift.

Plays the "Memory Waltz" when struck.
SIGNET RING
OF THE OSTREGAS
Cabinet 6 — Ring, Rare
Effect: While wearing this ring, you have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks when interacting with nobility or those who recognize the Ostrega family name.

Attunement: Not required

Bears the family crest in tarnished gold.
VIAL OF YACKLE'S BLOOD
Cabinet 6 — Potion Component
Effect: This vial contains blood that glows faintly green. Can be used as a material component for resurrection magic or consumed (as an action) to gain resistance to necrotic damage for 1 hour.

One Use Only
CLOCKWORK AMULET
Cabinet 7 — Amulet, Common
Effect: Once per day, when you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can use this amulet to treat the d20 roll as a 10 (before rolling).

Attunement: Not required

"Time is a machine. Machines can be stopped."
BAG OF BALL BEARINGS
Cabinet 7 — Equipment
Effect: As an action, scatter these on the ground to cover a 10-foot square. Any creature moving through must succeed on DC 10 Dexterity save or fall prone.

Contains 1,000 ball bearings.
WIND-UP FAMILIAR
Cabinet 7 — Magical Toy
Effect: Wind this mechanical mouse and it functions as a Find Familiar spell (mouse form only) for 1 hour. Doesn't require concentration. Obeys simple commands.

One Use Only (can be rewound with Mending)
RING OF SPELL STORING
Cabinet 8 — Ring, Rare
Effect: This ring currently contains one casting of Dispel Magic at 5th level. Any creature can cast the spell from the ring.

Special: Intended to be used against Yackle in combat!

Attunement: Required (for storing new spells, not for casting stored spell)
YACKLE'S TRUE NAME
Cabinet 8 — Knowledge
Effect: You learn Yackle's true name (GM decides what it is). Speaking her true name in combat forces her to make a DC 15 Wisdom save or lose concentration on all active spells.

Special: The parchment burns to ash after being read. Players must remember it!

Can only be used once per combat.
