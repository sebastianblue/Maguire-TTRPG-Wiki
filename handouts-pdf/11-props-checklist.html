SEAL PROPS (Physical items needed)

  • Seal 1 (Memory): 4 music boxes (or 4 small boxes labeled: Brass Ornate, Simple Wood, Painted Flowers, Black Lacquer)
  • Seal 2 (Blood): 8 textile swatches (fabric squares with patterns: dots, scales, stars, text, diamonds, dense flowers, waves, sparse flowers)
  • Seal 3 (Nature): 4 small books (label spines: Badger Memoirs, Wren Poetry, Foxwood Debates, Kinderly Letters) + 4 puzzle pieces that form a circle
  • Seal 4 (Service): Standard deck of playing cards (need: A♠, 2♥, 3♣, 4♦, 5♥, 6♠, 7♦, 8♣)
  • Seal 5 (Sacrifice): Telescope (toy/cardboard) that unscrews + box of matches hidden inside + REAL CANDLE on actual gaming table
  • Seal 6 (Deed): Masquerade mask (white/plain, player must physically wear it)
  • Seal 7 (Hunger): 8 name cards (printed from handout 06)
⚠️ CRITICAL: For Seal 5, you MUST have a real candle and matches at the gaming table. Players must physically light it!

CABINET SYSTEM PROPS

  • 8 small boxes/containers labeled Cabinet I-VIII (use printed labels from handout 08)
  • Cabinet 1: Star-shaped keyhole (cut from cardboard) + small toy/trinket as "key"
  • Cabinet 2: Combination lock (paper/cardboard with 3 rings showing: Skull, Hourglass, Feather)
  • Cabinet 3: "Frosted glass" (tissue paper over box)
  • Cabinet 4: Covered in small mirrors (aluminum foil or mirror stickers)
  • Cabinet 5: Make a sound when touched (bell inside, or crinkly paper)
  • Cabinet 6: Red wax seal (actual wax seal or red paper)
  • Cabinet 7: Gears visible (draw on box or glue plastic gears) + punch-card key
  • Cabinet 8: Different appearance each time (rotate which box you use, or have 3-4 different small boxes)

CABINET CONTENTS (Place inside boxes)

  • Cabinet 1: Healing potion card + scroll
  • Cabinet 2: Ring (toy ring or index card saying "Ring of Necrotic Resistance")
  • Cabinet 3: Cloak/cloth + bottle (empty small bottle labeled "Smoke")
  • Cabinet 4: Amulet + small mirror shard
  • Cabinet 5: Instrument (harmonica/recorder/toy) or instrument card
  • Cabinet 6: Ring + vial (small bottle with colored water labeled "Yackle's Blood") + Family Tree printout
  • Cabinet 7: Clockwork amulet (wind-up toy or card) + ball bearings + toy mouse
  • Cabinet 8: Yackle's Last Letter (handout 03) + I LOVE U carving (handout 07) + "Yackle's True Name" (write on paper that players must burn after reading) + Ring of Spell Storing card

OTHER PROPS

  • 8 pre-made character sheets (pregen characters)
  • Copies of character secrets (give to players privately)
  • Timer/phone for tracking 8-hour timeline
  • Initiative tracker
  • Yackle miniature/token (for final battle)
  • Grid/battle map (for Dining Room final encounter)

SETUP ORDER

  1. Print all handouts, cut out name cards and cabinet labels
  2. Assemble 8 cabinet boxes, attach labels, hide contents inside
  3. Prepare 4 music boxes for Seal 1, hide Simple Wood as correct answer
  4. Cut 8 fabric swatches for Seal 2
  5. Prepare 4 books with puzzle pieces inside for Seal 3
  6. Have deck of cards ready for Seal 4
  7. Prepare telescope with matches hidden inside + real candle on table for Seal 5
  8. Have masquerade mask ready for Seal 6
  9. Place name cards at dining table for Seal 7
  10. Place all cabinets in "Yackle's Study" area
  11. Have Will ready to read at start of session
✓ TIP: Put each seal's props in a labeled plastic bag for easy organization during play!
