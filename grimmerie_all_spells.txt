<h1>Grimmerie Tradition Spells</h1>

<p class="flavor-text">"The Grimmerie speaks in tongues of fire and shadow, accessible only to those willing to pay its price."</p>

<p>The Grimmerie Tradition represents the most powerful and dangerous arcane magic in Oz. These spells are primarily drawn from <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('objects/magic-items/artifacts/the-grimmerie', 'the Grimmerie'); return false;">the Grimmerie</a> itself, the ancient book of forbidden magic. Available to <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/classes/the-witch', 'Witches'); return false;">Witches</a> and <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/classes/the-adept', 'Adepts'); return false;">Adepts</a> who can decode its pages.</p>

<h2>Cantrips (0-Circle)</h2>

<p><strong>Abjur</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Eldritch Ward</strong> - You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to one damage type of your choice: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Conj</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Conjure Flame</strong> - A flickering flame appears in your hand. The flame sheds bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. The flame can be used to ignite flammable objects.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Div</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Read Intent</strong> - You touch a creature. For the duration, you have advantage on Wisdom (Insight) checks to determine if the creature is lying.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ench</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Compel Truth</strong> - You speak a word of power. One creature you can see within 30 feet must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be unable to speak a deliberate lie for 1 minute.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Evoc</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Eldritch Blast</strong> - A beam of crackling green energy streaks toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 force damage. The spell creates additional beams at higher levels (2 at 5th, 3 at 11th, 4 at 17th).</li>
<li><strong>Ray of Frost</strong> - A frigid beam of blue-white light streaks toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, it takes 1d8 cold damage, and its speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Illus</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>False Face</strong> - You cause your features to shift subtly. For 1 hour, you have advantage on Charisma (Deception) checks made to disguise your identity.</li>
<li><strong>Minor Illusion</strong> - You create a sound or an image of an object within range that lasts for the duration. The illusion ends if you dismiss it or cast this spell again.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Necro</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Chill Touch</strong> - You create a ghostly, skeletal hand that reaches out. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 necrotic damage and can't regain hit points until the start of your next turn.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Trans</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Prestidigitation</strong> - A minor magical trick. You can create sensory effects, light/snuff candles, clean/soil objects, chill/warm material, or make marks appear.</li>
<li><strong>Mage Hand</strong> - A spectral hand appears and can manipulate objects, open unlocked doors, or retrieve items within 30 feet.</li>
</ul>

<h2>1st-Circle Spells</h2>

<p><strong>Abjur</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Shield</strong> - An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC.</li>
<li><strong>Protection from Scrying</strong> - You ward one willing creature you touch. For 8 hours, divination magic can't target that creature or perceive them through scrying sensors.</li>
<li><strong>Mage Armor</strong> - You touch a willing creature who isn't wearing armor. Until the spell ends, the target's base AC becomes 13 + its Dexterity modifier.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Conj</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Find Familiar</strong> - You gain the service of a familiar, a spirit that takes an animal form you choose. The familiar can deliver touch spells and acts independently but obeys your commands.</li>
<li><strong>Grease</strong> - Slick grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square. Each creature in the area must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or fall prone.</li>
<li><strong>Fog Cloud</strong> - You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog. The area is heavily obscured.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Div</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Identify</strong> - You learn the properties of one magical item or the spell affecting one creature or object.</li>
<li><strong>Detect Magic</strong> - For 10 minutes, you sense the presence of magic within 30 feet and can determine its school.</li>
<li><strong>Comprehend Languages</strong> - For 1 hour, you understand any spoken language you hear and can read any written language you see.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ench</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Charm Person</strong> - You attempt to charm a humanoid you can see. It must make a Wisdom saving throw, and does so with advantage if you or your companions are fighting it. If it fails, it is charmed for 1 hour.</li>
<li><strong>Sleep</strong> - Creatures within 20 feet of a point you choose fall unconscious in order of their current hit points, starting with the lowest, up to 5d8 total hit points.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Evoc</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Burning Hands</strong> - A thin sheet of flames shoots from your outstretched fingertips. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.</li>
<li><strong>Magic Missile</strong> - You create three glowing darts of magical force. Each dart hits a creature of your choice that you can see within range, dealing 1d4 + 1 force damage.</li>
<li><strong>Thunderwave</strong> - A wave of thunderous force sweeps out from you. Each creature in a 15-foot cube must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 2d8 thunder damage on a failure and being pushed 10 feet away.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Illus</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Disguise Self</strong> - You make yourself—including clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings—look different for 1 hour or until you dismiss it.</li>
<li><strong>Silent Image</strong> - You create the image of an object, a creature, or some other visible phenomenon that is no larger than a 15-foot cube.</li>
<li><strong>Color Spray</strong> - A dazzling array of flashing, colored light springs from your hand. Roll 6d10; creatures in a 15-foot cone are blinded in order of their hit points, starting with the lowest.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Necro</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Cause Fear</strong> - You awaken the sense of mortality in one creature you can see. It must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you until the spell ends.</li>
<li><strong>Ray of Sickness</strong> - Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 poison damage and must make a Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of your next turn.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Trans</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Feather Fall</strong> - Choose up to five falling creatures within range. A falling creature's rate of descent slows to 60 feet per round until the spell ends.</li>
<li><strong>Expeditious Retreat</strong> - You can take the Dash action as a bonus action on each of your turns for the duration (10 minutes).</li>
<li><strong>Jump</strong> - You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled for 1 minute.</li>
</ul>

<h2>2nd-Circle Spells</h2>

<p><strong>Abjur</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Arcane Lock</strong> - You touch a closed door, window, gate, chest, or other entryway, and it becomes locked for the duration. You and creatures you designate can open it normally.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Conj</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Misty Step</strong> - Briefly surrounded by silvery mist, you teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see.</li>
<li><strong>Cloud of Daggers</strong> - You fill the air with spinning daggers in a 5-foot cube. A creature takes 4d4 slashing damage when it enters the area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Div</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Detect Thoughts</strong> - You can read the surface thoughts of creatures within 30 feet. The target can make a Wisdom saving throw to resist.</li>
<li><strong>See Invisibility</strong> - For 1 hour, you see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible.</li>
<li><strong>Locate Object</strong> - Sense the direction to an object you describe that is within 1,000 feet.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ench</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Hold Person</strong> - Choose a humanoid you can see. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be paralyzed for the duration (up to 1 minute).</li>
<li><strong>Suggestion</strong> - You suggest a course of activity and magically influence a creature you can see. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw or pursue the course of action you described.</li>
<li><strong>Crown of Madness</strong> - One humanoid you can see must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, it is charmed and must use its action to make a melee attack against a creature other than itself that you mentally choose.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Evoc</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Scorching Ray</strong> - You create three rays of fire and hurl them at targets within range. Make a ranged spell attack for each ray. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage.</li>
<li><strong>Shatter</strong> - A sudden loud ringing noise erupts from a point of your choice within range. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius sphere must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 3d8 thunder damage on a failed save.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Illus</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Invisibility</strong> - A creature you touch becomes invisible until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is invisible as long as it is on the target's person.</li>
<li><strong>Mirror Image</strong> - Three illusory duplicates of yourself appear. Each time a creature targets you with an attack, roll a d20 to determine whether the attack targets you or an illusion.</li>
<li><strong>Blur</strong> - Your body becomes blurred. For the duration, any creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against you.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Necro</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Ray of Enfeeblement</strong> - A black beam of enervating energy springs from your finger. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target deals only half damage with weapon attacks that use Strength.</li>
<li><strong>Blindness/Deafness</strong> - You can blind or deafen a foe. The target must make a Constitution saving throw or be blinded or deafened (your choice) for 1 minute.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Trans</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Levitate</strong> - One creature or object of your choice rises vertically, up to 20 feet, and remains suspended there for the duration.</li>
<li><strong>Alter Self</strong> - You assume a different form. You can change your appearance, adapt to an aquatic environment, or grow natural weapons.</li>
<li><strong>Enlarge/Reduce</strong> - You cause a creature or object to grow larger or smaller for the duration.</li>
</ul>

<h2>3rd-Circle Spells</h2>

<p><strong>Abjur</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Counterspell</strong> - You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell. If the spell is 3rd circle or lower, it fails. For higher circles, make an ability check.</li>
<li><strong>Dispel Magic</strong> - Any spell of 3rd circle or lower on a target ends. For higher circles, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability.</li>
<li><strong>Remove Curse</strong> - All curses affecting one creature or object end.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Conj</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Conjure Animals</strong> - You summon fey spirits that take the form of beasts. They appear in unoccupied spaces you can see and obey your commands.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Div</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Clairvoyance</strong> - You create an invisible sensor within range in a location familiar to you or in an obvious unfamiliar location.</li>
<li><strong>Tongues</strong> - A creature you touch can understand any spoken language it hears, and when it speaks, any creature that knows at least one language can understand it.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ench</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fear</strong> - You project a phantasmal image of a creature's worst fears. Each creature in a 30-foot cone must make a Wisdom saving throw or drop what it's holding and become frightened.</li>
<li><strong>Hypnotic Pattern</strong> - You create a twisting pattern of colors. Each creature in a 30-foot cube must make a Wisdom saving throw or become charmed and incapacitated.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Evoc</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fireball</strong> - A bright streak flashes from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range and blossoms into a roaring explosion. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.</li>
<li><strong>Lightning Bolt</strong> - A stroke of lightning forming a line 100 feet long and 5 feet wide blasts from you. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d6 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Illus</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Major Image</strong> - You create the image of an object, a creature, or some other visible phenomenon that is no larger than a 20-foot cube. The image includes sound, smell, and temperature appropriate to the thing depicted.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Necro</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Animate Dead</strong> - This spell creates an undead servant from a corpse. The creature becomes a skeleton or zombie (your choice) and obeys your commands.</li>
<li><strong>Vampiric Touch</strong> - Make a melee spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 3d6 necrotic damage, and you regain hit points equal to half the necrotic damage dealt.</li>
<li><strong>Speak with Dead</strong> - You grant the semblance of life to a corpse, allowing it to answer up to five questions.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Trans</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fly</strong> - You touch a willing creature. The target gains a flying speed of 60 feet for the duration (10 minutes).</li>
<li><strong>Haste</strong> - Choose a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's speed is doubled, it gains a +2 bonus to AC, has advantage on Dexterity saving throws, and gains an additional action on each of its turns.</li>
<li><strong>Slow</strong> - You alter time around up to six creatures. Each target must make a Wisdom saving throw or be affected by slow, having its speed halved and suffering other penalties.</li>
</ul>

<h2>4th-Circle Spells</h2>

<p><strong>Abjur</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Banishment</strong> - You attempt to send one creature to another plane of existence. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be banished.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Conj</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dimension Door</strong> - You teleport yourself and up to one willing creature to a spot within 500 feet.</li>
<li><strong>Conjure Minor Elementals</strong> - You summon elementals that appear in unoccupied spaces and obey your commands.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Div</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Arcane Eye</strong> - You create an invisible, magical eye that hovers in the air for the duration. You mentally receive visual information from the eye.</li>
<li><strong>Locate Creature</strong> - Describe or name a creature familiar to you. You sense the direction to the creature's location if it is within 1,000 feet.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ench</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dominate Beast</strong> - You attempt to beguile a beast. It must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be charmed by you for the duration.</li>
<li><strong>Compulsion</strong> - Each creature of your choice in a 30-foot cube must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, it must move in a direction you choose on each of its turns.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Evoc</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Ice Storm</strong> - Hail falls in a 20-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder. Each creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 bludgeoning damage and 4d6 cold damage on a failure.</li>
<li><strong>Wall of Fire</strong> - You create a wall of fire on a solid surface. One side deals 5d8 fire damage to creatures within 10 feet.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Illus</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Greater Invisibility</strong> - You or a creature you touch becomes invisible until the spell ends. Attacking or casting spells doesn't end the invisibility.</li>
<li><strong>Hallucinatory Terrain</strong> - You make natural terrain in a 150-foot cube look, sound, and smell like some other sort of terrain.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Necro</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Blight</strong> - Necromantic energy washes over a creature you can see. The target must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 8d8 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Trans</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Polymorph</strong> - You transform a creature into a beast with a CR equal to or less than the target's level or CR. The target's statistics are replaced by those of the beast.</li>
<li><strong>Stone Shape</strong> - You touch a stone object or section of stone no larger than Medium and form it into any shape that suits your purpose.</li>
</ul>

<h2>5th-Circle Spells</h2>

<p><strong>Abjur</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Wall of Force</strong> - An invisible wall of force springs into existence. Nothing can physically pass through the wall.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Conj</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Teleportation Circle</strong> - You create a circle on the ground. When you finish casting, a portal opens, linking your location with a permanent teleportation circle whose sigil sequence you know.</li>
<li><strong>Conjure Elemental</strong> - You summon an elemental servant that appears in an unoccupied space.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Div</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Scrying</strong> - You can see and hear a particular creature you choose. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw, modified by how well you know it.</li>
<li><strong>Contact Other Plane</strong> - You mentally contact a demigod, elemental prince, or cosmic entity and ask up to five questions. You risk madness if your Intelligence is too low.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Ench</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dominate Person</strong> - You attempt to beguile a humanoid. It must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be charmed and under your control.</li>
<li><strong>Modify Memory</strong> - You attempt to reshape another creature's memories. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, you can modify up to 10 minutes of its memories.</li>
<li><strong>Geas</strong> - You place a magical command on a creature. It must carry out some service or refrain from some action, or take 5d10 psychic damage each day it doesn't obey.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Evoc</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Cone of Cold</strong> - A blast of cold air erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 8d8 cold damage on a failure.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Necro</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Cloudkill</strong> - You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of poisonous yellow-green fog. The fog spreads around corners and is heavily obscuring. Each creature in the area takes 5d8 poison damage.</li>
<li><strong>Animate Objects</strong> - Objects come to life at your command. Choose up to ten nonmagical objects that are not being worn or carried. Medium targets count as two, Large as four, Huge as eight.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Unique Grimmerie Spells</h2>
<p>The following spells exist only in <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('objects/magic-items/artifacts/the-grimmerie', 'the Grimmerie'); return false;">the Grimmerie</a> and represent its most dangerous secrets:</p>

<p><strong>5th Circle</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Awaken Animal Consciousness</strong> - <em>8 hour casting time.</em> You touch a beast with Intelligence 3 or less. Its Intelligence becomes 10, and it gains speech and one skill proficiency. Unlike standard *awaken*, this transformation is permanent and hereditary. This spell created the <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/races/animals/overview', 'Animals'); return false;">Animals</a> of Oz. Using it is considered heresy by the <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/classes/the-unionist', 'Unionist Church'); return false;">Unionist Church</a>.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3rd Circle</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Silence the Voiced</strong> - Choose one <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('character/races/animals/overview', 'Animal'); return false;">Animal</a> within 60 feet. It must make a Wisdom saving throw or permanently lose its ability to speak. The creature retains intelligence but cannot form words or cast spells with verbal components. Can be removed with *greater restoration* or *wish*.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2nd Circle</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Green Elixir</strong> - <em>1 hour casting time.</em> You brew the mysterious green potion. A creature that drinks it makes a DC 15 Constitution save. On success, gain advantage on one check within 1 hour. On failure, roll d6: (1) if pregnant, child born green-skinned with magical ability, (2-6) various temporary magical effects. This spell affected <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('npcs/elphaba-thropp', 'Elphaba\'s'); return false;">Elphaba's</a> mother.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>7th Circle</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Time Fracture</strong> - You cause temporal distortions in a 30-foot radius. Time moves erratically; creatures must make Wisdom saves or become disoriented, gaining disadvantage on all rolls. Some effects may persist as <a href="#" onclick="Tabs.openTab('magic/phenomena/time-slippage', 'Time Slippage'); return false;">time anomalies</a>.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>9th Circle</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Soul Transfer</strong> - You attempt to move a willing soul from one body to another prepared vessel. Both vessels must be present. Requires intense concentration and carries risk of permanent soul damage. This is theoretical even within the Grimmerie's pages.</li>
</ul>
